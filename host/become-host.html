Frequently Asked Questions

Who can be a host on JCP Air BnB?

+

Anyone who has a space to share can become a host. Whether you have a spare room, an entire home, or a unique space, you can list it on JCP Air BnB.

How much does it cost to list my space?

+

It's free to create a listing on JCP Air BnB. Once you receive bookings, we charge a service fee of 3% of the booking subtotal.

How do I set my price?

+

You can set your own price for your listing. Our pricing tool can help you set a competitive rate based on similar listings in your area, demand, and other factors.

How do I get paid?

+

You'll get paid via direct deposit about 24 hours after your guest's scheduled check-in time. You can set up your payment details in your account settings.
